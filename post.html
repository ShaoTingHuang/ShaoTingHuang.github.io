Challenge myself - heading to my dreams

Finally I got the diploma from CMU

Posted by Shao Ting Huang on May 20, 2017

Many people asked me why I disappeared, and now finally I can share it!

My life in CMU is like passing two years in one year. In order to save money, I took library shifts, slept in the living room, and cooked for myself. I worked very hard and strengthen my coding skills everyday. Life seems boring but in fact it’s really crazy and fulfilling!

I only studied two semesters in CMU. I took machine learning and data structure to build the foundation of coding in the first semester. In the last semester, I challenged myself, taking cloud computing, interactive data science, and audited web-application.

What was most significant about the lunar voyage was not that man set foot on the Moon but that they set eye on the earth.

The most interesting part of learning in CMU is doing project of cloud computing. I spent nearly 50 hours to achieve every project. In this course, I understood what is self-learning. I needed to analyze problem, and try to find the method to solve it from the limitless resource - website. In cloud computing course, I really experienced what is “Just try it, and let your hand dirty”.

Indeed, I came here for learning coding. The passion was ignited from the entrepreneurship contest when I was senior in college. I saw the power of realizing idea with computer and the beauty of works from my teammates. I didn’t know how to code but I thought of an idea to realize program trading.

How I improved coding ability in only one year? I achieved 89% profit from analyzing stock market with programming. Though it’s a small capital, I know I am heading to my dream.
